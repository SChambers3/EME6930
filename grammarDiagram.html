Pattern 1 & 2

Pattern 1 is noun-verb (subject-verb) sentence pattern. Pattern 2 is a noun-verb-noun (subject-verb-direct object) sentence pattern. The second noun is the Direct Object of the sentence.


Pattern 3

Pattern 3 is a noun-verb-noun-noun (subject-verb-indirect object-direct object) sentence pattern. It includes both the direct object and an indirect object.


Pattern 4 & 5

Patten 4 is a noun-linking verb-noun (subject-linking verb- predicate nominative) sentence pattern. Patten 5 is a noun-linking verb-adjective (subject-linking verb- predicate adjective) sentence pattern.
